BigComChat

A workspace for ecommerce professionals working with BigCommerce

Join us

What are the benefits?

  • An open forum for store owners, consultants, developers and other experts to share ideas, ask for feedback and network.
  • Stay in the loop with BigCommerce news and updates.
  • Private "merchant-only", "agency-only" and "tech-partner-only" channels.
  • Watercooler chat with others in your industry

Why a slack channel?

There are other community spaces that provide value to their members. For example, the BigCommerce Devs Slack, Facebook group, and forum. However, only developers are allowed in the official Slack community, some people prefer not to use Facebook, and forums aren't geared towards community discussions.

We believe there's an unmet need for a pseudo-asynchronous community space suitable for all professionals working with BigCommerce daily to support knowledge exchange & community growth.

Who's involved?

Individuals within the community started this initiative and it is not owned by any single company.
